BpkSpinner

open class BpkSpinner : FrameLayout (source)

BpkSpinner is designed to indicate that a part of the product is loading or performing a task when the amount of time needed is unknown.

To further customize the visual of the progress progressBarStyle and progressBarStyleSmall should be used:

<style name="AppTheme" parent="Theme.AppCompat.Light.NoActionBar">
  <item name="android:progressBarStyle">@style/CustomProgress</item>
</style>

<style name="CustomProgress" parent="@style/Widget.AppCompat.ProgressBar">
  <item name="android:minWidth">200dp</item>
</style>
